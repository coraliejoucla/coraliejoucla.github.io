Publications

{% capture publis_text %} I value [open science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_science) as a way to make research more transparent, accessible, and collaborative. Whenever possible, I publish in open access and share data or analysis tools so others can reuse and build on my work. This approach not only accelerates discovery but also strengthens trust in research by making it verifiable and usable beyond disciplinary and institutional boundaries. Open practices not only strengthen reproducibility but also broaden the impact of research, helping knowledge reach beyond academic circles and benefit a wider community.

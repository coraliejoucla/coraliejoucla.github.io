Music

As a musician, arranger and project leader, I enjoy exploring music as both an artistic and collective experience. My work spans orchestral performance and arrangement, with a particular focus on symphonic and cinematic repertoires. Over the years, I have combined artistic practice with project coordination and large-scale collective productions within university and associative orchestras.

Core skills Orchestra Creation & arrangements Project management Media portfolio

Core skills

Began playing the transverse flute at the age of 7, 3rd cycle diploma from the French Music Confederation in 2016

Then continued in Pierre-Simon Chevry's class from 2018 to 2022

Also plays the piccolo

Started violin classes in 2024, 1st cycle

Orchestra experience

Besançon University symphonic orchestra, 8 years

  • First flute in the orchestra, regularly performing orchestral solos
  • Solo singer on a symphonic version of Paradis blanc (Michel Berger), performed at two concerts in 2023

Besançon University big band, 4 years

Wind orchestras (Delle and Etupes, 6 years)

Green Lemon: irish folk ensemble (violins, cello, flute, harp)

Projet MarFlu: flute-Marimba duo founded in 2024 with Rémi Meyer, exploring the expressive potential of a rare instrumental pairing. Projet MarFlu highlights both instruments as equal partners, bridging classical repertoire and contemporary concert programming.

  • Programme I — Personal favourites and transcriptions, including Fantaisie pastorale hongroise by Franz Doppler, specially arranged for marimba
  • Programme II (Dance) — Serbian dances, tangos, and Danse macabre by Camille Saint-Saëns

Creation & arrangements

Large-scale orchestral projects (2024 — Video Game Music)

  • Arrangements for symphonic orchestra, big band, and joint symphonic + big band formations
  • Small and mixed ensembles: brass quintet, wind quintet, string quintet, electric guitars & drums, chamber groups

Big band and pop music (ex: Shabby by La Fanfare en pétard)

Musical theatre: 2 numbers from the musical Big! for a dedicated musical-theatre orchestra

Folk & Trad music: pieces for Green Lemon, an Irish folk ensemble (two violins, cello, flute, harp), in which I perform

Project Management — Symphonic Orchestra

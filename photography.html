Photography

As a passionate photographer, I love telling stories through images. My work explores both travel and the stage, with a particular focus on light and emotions. I have covered Broadway-style shows and musicals for several years, and was given an exhibition to showcase my work on my trip to the United States.

Note: this is only a small sample of my work. Furthermore, for obvious safety reasons, child actors' faces have been blurred.
